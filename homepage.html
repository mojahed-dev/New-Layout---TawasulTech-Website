  • Agree on transparent pricing and service levels

Fleet Management System (FMS)

A connected fleet operations layer for AVLs, trailers, and assets. Combine live tracking, CAN-bus data, and service workflows in a single operational view.

  • Real-time GPS tracking for vehicles and trailers
  • Engine, odometer, and fuel data from CAN-bus / FMS
  • Configurable alerts for idling, speeding, and misuse
  • Maintenance planning by mileage, hours, or dates
  • Dashboards for fleet utilization and cost per km
Explore FMS

AI & Driver Safety Telematics

Strengthen road safety with driver-centric analytics. Combine trip scoring, harsh-event detection, and coaching workflows to reward safer driving habits.

  • Harsh braking, acceleration, and cornering detection
  • Driver scorecards by trip, day, and fleet segment
  • Exception reports for high-risk drivers and routes
  • Optional in-cab alerts and coaching notifications
  • Evidence exports for safety and insurance teams

Smart Waste Operations (SWMS)

An operations system for waste haulers and municipalities. Optimize collection routes, container pickups, and field crews with live status and SLAs.

  • Digitized work orders for collection and sweeping
  • Configurable routes with live truck progress
  • Container inventory with RFID / QR tagging options
  • Service-level tracking per zone, customer, or contract
  • Operations reports for regulators and municipalities
