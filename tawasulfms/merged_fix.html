Benefits Of The Tawasul System

Productivity Increase

Complete tasks faster and more proactively. Monitor drivers and ensure they perform their tasks to the fullest.

Customer Satisfaction

The system helps identify the nearest vehicle to a customer location, improving service quality and speed, while also enhancing productivity and leading to faster completion.

Operating Cost Reduction

We help you keep a close eye on every part of your business to ensure everything runs smoothly with reduced operating costs.

Protection

The system helps monitor and regulate driver behavior to protect them and others, as well as assets and vehicles, for safe driving.

Fuel Cost Reduction

Amid rising fuel prices, reducing fuel consumption is a key goal, achieved by monitoring speed, controlling tasks, and eliminating unjustified activities, leading to cost savings.

Routing

The system guides employees to reach locations and complete assigned tasks using mobile apps that smoothly and proactively direct them to customers.

Work Hours Control

The system provides tools for controlling work hours, such as trip reports, time logs, and driver records, to track actual work hours and identify wasted time.

Insurance Cost Reduction

Monitoring driver behavior and vehicle operation reduces accidents and traffic violations, resulting in lower annual insurance premiums for vehicles.
