Waste fill-level monitoring

Sensoneo Waste Monitoring solution allows cities and businesses to uncover and understand the complex dynamics of their waste production. The solution provides real-time online access to data related to monitored bins, boxes, or containers. Waste Monitoring solution combines Smart Sensors, Smart Waste Management Software System, and Citizen App.

The Smart Sensors and Monitors use ultrasound technology to measure the fill levels (along with other data) in bins several times a day. Using a variety of IoT networks (Sigfox, NB-IoT, LoRaWAN, GPRS), the sensors send the data to Sensoneo’s Smart Waste Management Software System, a powerful cloud-based platform for data-driven daily operations, available also as a waste management app.

Customers are provided data-driven decision making, and optimization of waste collection routes, frequencies, and vehicle loads. We focus on tailored solutions—share your specific challenges with us so we can assess the benefits our solution can bring to your waste management processes.

Intelligent Analytics

Our analytics tool captures ‘big data’ to help make informed decisions. It generates statistics, reports, and measurements about containers, contents, and collection paths. By consolidating data from sensors, vehicles, and operational systems, the platform provides real-time visibility into waste operations across the city. Municipal teams can monitor performance trends, identify inefficiencies, and optimize collection strategies with confidence. Clear dashboards and actionable insights support proactive planning, improve service reliability, and enable cost-effective, scalable waste management for smart city environments.

Smart Waste Management Software System

The powerful cloud-based waste management software system enables the customer to configure, monitor and manage daily operations of a waste management company. In addition, to live data from Waste monitoring, the tool can also hold complex bin database, plan the optimal collection routes, predict filling cycles, and manage reports from employees and citizens. The solution is hosted in the Microsoft Azure cloud, belongs to one of the most modern waste management software on the market.

Smart Sensors and Monitors

Smart Sensors and Monitors are designed for monitoring fill level in smart trash bins and containers using ultrasonic technology. Sensors and Monitors transfer data very simple via all currently available IoT networks and/or GPRS. Sensors monitor all types of waste in bins and containers of different sizes. They are robust, water and shock-resistant. Fire alarm, tilt recognition and other features are included.

Citizen Application

Citizens app provides access to data from Smart Sensors and Monitors to citizens. The mobile app informs about the location and fill level of monitored bins and enables you to find the nearest available bin for disposal of garbage or report an issue. Logged users can access even more information about the bins, request a pickup or maintenance. Available for free on Android and iOS.
